The Problem

Hiring teams fly blind after every interview.

  • Feedback scattered across email threads, Slack messages, and interviewers' heads
  • No consistent scoring — every interviewer uses a different mental scale
  • No single place to track how a candidate is tracking across multiple rounds
  • Result: conflicting opinions, bias creep, bad hires — or missed talent
✓

The Solution

One structured workflow from session to decision.

  • Every interviewer scores against the same rubric — communication, technical fit, culture, role competencies
  • Feedback consolidates into a single panel view — no chasing people down
  • Hiring managers see real-time session status and reviewer completion at a glance
  • Decisions grounded in evidence, not scattered notes or gut feel
