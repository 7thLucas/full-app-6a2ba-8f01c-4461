# interviewqqq — Product Overview

## What It Is
interviewqqq is an interview session reviewer app that gives hiring teams full visibility into how their interviews are going. It centralizes session review, structured scoring, and panel feedback so hiring decisions are grounded in evidence — not scattered notes or gut feel.

## The Problem
Hiring teams fly blind. After interviews, feedback lives in email threads, Slack messages, or interviewers' heads. There's no structured way to review sessions, no consistent scoring, and no single place to see how a candidate is tracking across multiple interviewers. The result: conflicting opinions, bias creep, and bad hires — or missed talent.

## The Solution
InterviewIQ gives every interviewer a structured session review workflow: score the candidate, log observations against a rubric, and submit feedback that the whole panel can see in one place. Hiring managers get a real-time view of how every session is going without chasing people down.

## Users
- **Hiring managers** — need an overview of all active candidates and panel opinions
- **Recruiters** — coordinate the process, track completion of session reviews
- **Panel interviewers** — conduct sessions and submit structured feedback

## Core Features
1. **Session Review** — Log and review individual interview sessions with structured fields (candidate, role, date, interviewer, notes)
2. **Scoring Rubric** — Score candidates on defined criteria (communication, technical fit, culture, role-specific competencies)
3. **Panel Feedback Dashboard** — See all reviewer scores and comments for a candidate in one consolidated view
4. **Session History** — Browse past sessions and track candidate progression across rounds
5. **Feedback Completion Tracking** — Know which interviewers haven't submitted yet; send reminders

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: interviewqqq
- **Positioning**: The interview intelligence layer for hiring teams
- **Tone**: Analytical, clear, calm — no fluff; built for people making real decisions
- **Colors**: Deep navy primary (#1E3A5F), sky blue secondary (#0EA5E9), emerald accent (#10B981)
- **Feel**: Professional, data-informed, trustworthy

## Scope (MVP)
Web app. Core loop: create a session → score it → submit feedback → view consolidated panel results. No video recording or transcription in MVP — review is text/structured form based.

## Strategic Principles
- Every fact displayed comes from an interviewer's direct input — no AI inferences on candidate quality
- Panel visibility is the core unlock: one place, all opinions, no chasing
- Keep the review form short enough to complete in 5 minutes post-interview
